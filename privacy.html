Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

Storybook Stuffies is a children's interactive storybook app designed for ages 3-7. We take your child's privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Game Progress: Story completion, character progress, outfit unlocks
  • Mushroom Currency Balance: In-game currency earned through interactions
  • Settings: Audio preferences, tutorial hint preferences
  • CloudKit Sync Data: If you choose to sync across devices, the above data is stored in your private iCloud account

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ No personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No photos or camera access
  • ❌ No contact information
  • ❌ No device identifiers for tracking
  • ❌ No behavioral analytics
  • ❌ No third-party advertising networks
  • ❌ No social media integration

How We Use Information

The only data we store is your child's game progress, which is used to:

  • Save story completion and unlock progress
  • Remember currency earned and items purchased
  • Sync progress across your devices via your private iCloud account (if enabled)

This data never leaves your control. It is stored either locally on your device or in your private iCloud account.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Storybook Stuffies is designed for children ages 3-7 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not allow children to share personal information
  • We do not use persistent identifiers for tracking
  • We do not include third-party advertising
  • We do not have social features or chat capabilities
  • Parents have full control over all app features via Parent Settings

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game progress is stored securely on your device
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, data syncs to your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit
  • No External Servers: We do not operate servers or databases that collect your information
  • Content Delivery: Story content is delivered via GitHub Pages (public CDN) but does not collect or track usage

Third-Party Services

Apple CloudKit (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your game progress syncs to your private iCloud storage
  • This data is controlled by your Apple ID and iCloud settings
  • Apple's Privacy Policy applies: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

GitHub Pages (Content Delivery)

  • We host story images and content on GitHub Pages (a public CDN)
  • No personal information is transmitted
  • No tracking or analytics are performed
  • This service simply delivers story content to the app

Apple App Store

  • In-app purchases are processed by Apple
  • Apple's privacy policies apply to purchase transactions
  • We do not receive or store payment information

Parental Rights and Controls

Parents can:

  • Access all app settings via the Parent Settings section (behind parent gate)
  • View current game progress and currency balance
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time via device settings
  • Delete all app data by deleting the app
  • Contact us with questions or concerns at support@stuffiesstorybook.com

App Store Ratings

The app may prompt you to rate it on the App Store. This uses Apple's standard rating system and does not collect any information beyond what Apple provides.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

To delete all app data:

  1. Delete the Storybook Stuffies app from your device
  2. If iCloud sync is enabled, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Storybook Stuffies → Delete Documents and Data
